LIMESTONE CARVING EXPERTS
Tulip limestone is a remarkable stone, ranging from a light buff to a bluish-gray in color. It’s ease of workability and capacity to accept and retain fine details, make it a favorite among stone carvers. Today’s carvers are a diverse group of artists. Some are born in to it, still working in the industrial side of the business alongside or in the tradition of their fathers, uncles or brothers who have done the same.
ORDERING INFORMATION When placing an order for a specialty carving, we require the following:
+ Drawings
+ Specifications of any full size layouts that are available
+ If the piece will be a replica of an existing carving, photographs and measurements are required
